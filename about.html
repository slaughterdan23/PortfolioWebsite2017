About

Games, Guitars, and GUI

Dan is a web developer, game developer, musician, and entreprenuer. After graduating college in 2015 with a BS in Music Business and an AS in Sound Engineering, Dan turned his attention to technology and development. Always inspired by the art of web and game design, Dan began teaching himself the fundamentals of web and game design, and found a job at a software startup. Before long, Dan grew impatient waiting for his job to teach him the skills he wanted and decided to attend Tech Talent South in Phoenix, AZ.
